St Aloysius College

St. Aloysius (Deemed to be University)[1] is a private, coeducational, Jesuit university located in Mangalore, Karnataka, India. With a 2022–23 enrollment of 5,436 undergraduate students and 1,587 postgraduate students and 70 research scholars, the university specializes in academic programs in the humanities, commerce, science, technology, and management.
